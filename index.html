About Zynx Corporation

Zynx Corporation is building the next generation of accessible, privacy-first AI platforms — tools that put control back in the hands of individuals and builders, without gatekeepers or unnecessary complexity.

We create specialized AI solutions that solve real-world problems in practical, everyday contexts — from quick local errands to global trading, personal knowledge management, and on-device code generation. Every platform is designed with simplicity, local-first execution, strong privacy protections, and real user benefit at the core.

Registered in the United States — we are establishing our legal entity in the U.S. to support global scalability, access to international infrastructure, and long-term growth while continuing to serve users worldwide from day one.

Our Mission

To make powerful AI infrastructure available to everyone — not just large organizations — by delivering ethical, scalable, and user-owned tools that work offline when needed and respect personal data boundaries.

What We Stand For

  • Privacy by design — data stays under user control
  • Accessibility — powerful tools that don’t require deep technical knowledge
  • Local-first whenever possible — reduce dependency on cloud surveillance
  • Builder mindset — we create for people who want to create
  • Transparency — clear communication, no hidden black boxes
